Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

⚡ Quick Summary: We respect your privacy. We collect minimal data, use industry-standard encryption, and never sell your information. You own your data and can delete it anytime.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bolt Studios ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, including QuickNote Pro and any future apps (collectively, the "Apps").

By using our Apps, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password when you create an account
  • Content Data: Notes, text, images, and other content you create within the Apps
  • Profile Information: Optional information like profile picture or preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, crash reports
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics to improve our Apps

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access your contacts, call logs, or SMS messages
  • We do NOT track your location unless explicitly required by a feature
  • We do NOT read data from other apps on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To operate and maintain our Apps, sync your data across devices
  • Improve Experience: To understand how users interact with our Apps and make improvements
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Authentication and database storage
  • Cloudinary: Image and media storage
  • Data Location: Servers are located in secure data centers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication with Firebase
  • Limited employee access to user data

⚠️ Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our Apps:

  • Firebase: Authentication, database, and analytics
  • Cloudinary: Image hosting and delivery
  • Unity Ads: Ad serving (only non-personal data)

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: You can access all your data through the App
  • Edit: You can modify your information at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual items or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (coming soon)

6.2 Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the App and go to Settings
  2. Select "Account" → "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion
  4. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the App to function properly
  • Analytics: Aggregate usage statistics (anonymized)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use advertising or social media tracking pixels

9. Third-Party Links

Our Apps may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. International Users

Our Apps are operated from India. If you use our Apps from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India where our servers and databases are located.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for major changes

Your continued use of the Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@boltstudios.in
  • Contact Form: boltstudios.in/contact
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

🇮🇳 For Indian Users: This Privacy Policy complies with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable Indian privacy laws.

13. Your Consent

By using our Apps, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
