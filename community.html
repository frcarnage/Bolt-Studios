2. Expected Behavior

When interacting with Bolt Studios apps, services, or community spaces, we expect you to:

  • Be Respectful: Treat others as you would like to be treated
  • Be Constructive: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions in a positive manner
  • Be Honest: Share genuine experiences and truthful information
  • Be Supportive: Help fellow users when possible and celebrate community successes
  • Follow the Rules: Comply with our Terms of Service and applicable laws
  • Report Issues: Alert us to bugs, security concerns, or guideline violations

3. Prohibited Conduct

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in removal from our community or services:

3.1 Harmful Content

  • Harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Content promoting violence, self-harm, or dangerous activities
  • Sexually explicit, obscene, or inappropriate content
  • Child exploitation or endangerment of minors in any form

3.2 Illegal Activities

  • Illegal activities or promotion of illegal activities
  • Infringement of intellectual property rights
  • Fraud, scams, or deceptive practices
  • Unauthorized access to systems or accounts
  • Distribution of malware or harmful software

3.3 Disruptive Behavior

  • Spam, excessive self-promotion, or unsolicited advertising
  • Impersonation of others or misrepresentation of affiliation
  • Manipulation of our apps or services for unfair advantage
  • Deliberate spread of misinformation
  • Attempts to circumvent security measures or access restrictions

4. User-Generated Content

If you create or share content related to Bolt Studios (reviews, screenshots, videos, etc.):

  • You retain ownership of your content
  • Ensure your content complies with these guidelines
  • Do not share others' personal information without consent
  • Give appropriate credit when using others' work
  • Be honest about your experiences and any affiliations

5. Privacy and Data Protection

  • Respect the privacy of other users
  • Do not share personal information of others without permission
  • Report any privacy concerns or data breaches to us immediately
  • Review our Privacy Policy to understand how we handle data

6. Feedback and Feature Requests

We love hearing from our community! When providing feedback:

  • Be specific and constructive
  • Explain the problem or suggestion clearly
  • Understand that we may not implement every suggestion
  • Be patient‚Äîdevelopment takes time
  • Avoid demanding or entitled language

7. Bug Reports and Security

If you discover a bug or security vulnerability:

  • Report it to us at bolt-studios@outlook.com as soon as possible
  • Do not exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain
  • Do not publicly disclose security issues before we've addressed them
  • Provide detailed information to help us reproduce and fix the issue

8. Social Media and Online Presence

When engaging with us on social media platforms:

  • Follow platform-specific rules and guidelines
  • Tag us appropriately (@boltstudios.official, @BoltStudiosIN, @BoltStudios)
  • Use hashtags responsibly
  • Respect our brand identity and trademarks

9. Enforcement

We reserve the right to take appropriate action when guidelines are violated, including:

  • Warning or educating users about violations
  • Removing violating content
  • Temporarily suspending access to services
  • Permanently banning users from our services
  • Reporting serious violations to appropriate authorities

Enforcement decisions are made at our discretion and are final.

10. Reporting Violations

If you encounter behavior or content that violates these guidelines:

  • Email us at bolt-studios@outlook.com with details
  • Include screenshots or evidence if possible
  • Provide context about the violation
  • Do not engage with or retaliate against the violator

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly. Reporter identities will be kept confidential when possible.

11. Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was made in error:

  • Contact us at bolt-studios@outlook.com
  • Explain why you believe the decision was incorrect
  • Provide any relevant context or evidence
  • Wait for our review and response

12. Changes to Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines as our community grows and evolves. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Updates on our website
  • Notifications on our social media channels
  • Email announcements (when applicable)

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.
